Your Impact

Every Dollar Makes a Difference

When you donate to Companions of CPAS, you're not just giving money—you're giving hope. Your generosity directly funds life-saving services that keep pets with their families, where they belong.

$25 provides a spay/neuter surgery
$50 covers essential vaccinations for a pet
$100 supplies a month of pet food assistance
$250 helps with emergency veterinary care

Why We Need You

Keeping Families Together

Many families in your community face difficult choices when their pets need care. Your donation ensures that financial hardship doesn't force families to surrender their beloved companions. Together, we're building a community where every pet is cared for and every family has support.
