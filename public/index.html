Our Story

A small rescue with big-heart impact in your community.

Companions of CPAS walks alongside local families with practical help, clear guidance, and deep compassion—so pets can stay healthy, safe, and at home where they belong.

  • Compassion first — no judgment, just real support when things get hard.
  • Prevention over crisis — spay/neuter, vaccines, and food assistance before it becomes an emergency.
  • Rooted in your community — we know the roads, the families, and the stories behind every call for help.
  • Transparent 501(c)(3) — careful, local stewardship of every donation (EIN: 93‑2791656).
