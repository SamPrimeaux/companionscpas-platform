Our Mission

We step in where the second chance starts.

Companions of CPAS exists to give dogs at Caddo Parish Animal Services the opportunity they may not get otherwise. As a 100% volunteer-based nonprofit, every dollar is directed toward helping animals who need care, transport, visibility, and a safe path forward.

Critical medical care — heartworm treatment, surgery, urgent needs, and recovery support.
Transport access — helping dogs reach no-kill rescue partners where adoption demand is stronger.
Foster pathways — giving families an easy, supported way to say yes.

Some dogs arrive with stories they cannot tell.
