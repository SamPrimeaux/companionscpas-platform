Our mission

Companions of CPAS exists to support pet owners in the your community community by providing access to spay and neuter services, vaccinations, and pet food assistance—helping families keep their pets healthy, at home, and out of shelters, while preventing unwanted litters.

We are intentionally small, local, and relational. That means we know the roads, the families, and the stories behind each call for help.

Our values

  • Compassion first — No judgment, only support.
  • Prevention over crisis — Spay, neuter, vaccinate early.
  • Community roots — Local help for local families.
  • Dignity for all — Pets and people deserve respect.
  • Practical impact — Real help, not just promises.

Boots-on-the-ground support

We coordinate appointments, transport when possible, and help families navigate low-cost options instead of leaving them to figure it out alone.

Animals at the center

Every program decision is filtered through: “What does this mean for the actual dog or cat in this home?”

Transparent, relationship-based help

We don’t shame, lecture, or post people’s struggles online—ever. We simply show up, help, and follow through.
